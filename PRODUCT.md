# Clinica Dentaire Verde

Register: brand

A fictional dental clinic in Bucharest, Romania, built as a warm clinical landing page for adults, teens, children, and anxious patients who want careful dentistry without a cold hospital feeling.

Users are Bucharest residents from 25 to 55, parents choosing pediatric dental care, and professionals who want transparent treatment plans. They are practical, slightly anxious about dental visits, and reassured by clarity, calm copy, and visible human care.

Brand words: careful, grounded, restorative.

Anti-references: generic teal medical templates, glossy perfect-smile stock pages, card-heavy SaaS layouts, exaggerated luxury dentistry, sterile hospital blue.

Strategic principle: communicate trustworthy treatment through restraint, real texture, natural photography, and honest Romanian language.
